Lasagna

Desciption: This lasagna is just the BEST! Your picky kids will be begging you for seconds!

Ingredients:

  • noodles
  • sauce
  • onions
  • chicken

Steps:

  1. Cook pasta according to directions.
  2. Roast chicken and onions for 30 minutes at 350.
  3. Combine chicken, onions, and pasta together.
  4. Cover with sauce and enjoy!
